## Narrative Structure & Style

### The Performative Mandate

The reader's experience mirrors the protagonist's journey:
- **Act I**: Reader experiences fragmented consciousness directly
- **Act II**: Reader must assemble multiple perspectives
- **Act III**: Reader discovers connections and patterns
- **Act IV**: Reader sees integrated whole

**Success = Reader performs the integration work, not just observes it**

### Fragmentation Strategy

**Four Techniques**:
1. **Motif Threading**: Plant motifs across chapters (e.g., "Echo")
2. **Structural Symmetry**: Parallel structures readers recognize
3. **Conceptual Callbacks**: Reference earlier philosophical concepts
4. **Hidden Concordances**: Subtle connections that deepen meaning

### Writing Principles

From the **Writer's Guide**:

1. **Perspective Integrity**: Narrator knows only what they should know
2. **Show, Don't Tell**: Dramatize concepts through events
3. **Technical Precision**: Use exact terms when they matter
4. **Fragmentation as Feature**: Embed discoverable clues

### Quality Standards

Every chapter must:
- Work independently as narrative
- Contain 2-3 discoverable clues
- Maintain POV consistency
- Advance philosophical questions
- Reward careful reading
